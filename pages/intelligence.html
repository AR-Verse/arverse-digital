NOTE // 02 SEARCH

Keyword volume is not the same as commercial value. Effective SEO and search advertising begin by understanding why people search, what decision stage they are in and which queries are connected to meaningful business outcomes.

NOTE // 03 EXPERIENCE

Every interaction should create clarity, confidence or momentum. Navigation, page structure and calls to action should reduce friction and help users understand the next meaningful step.

NOTE // 04 COMMERCE

Conversion optimization begins before checkout or form submission. Trust, relevance, message clarity and a coherent user journey shape whether a visitor feels confident enough to take action.
