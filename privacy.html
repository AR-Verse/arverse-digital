POLICY STATE ONLINE
01 OVERVIEW

HOW THIS POLICY WORKS

This Privacy Policy applies to information handled through the ARVERSE website, project intake forms, business enquiries, direct communications and service-related interactions.

The exact information involved may depend on how you interact with ARVERSE and the nature of the service or project being discussed.

02 INFORMATION WE RECEIVE

INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE

When you submit a form or contact ARVERSE, you may provide information such as your name, email address, company name, region, business stage, requested services, estimated budget, target timeline and project details.

You may also provide additional information voluntarily through emails, meetings, calls or other business communication.

PROJECT INTAKE

Information submitted through our project intake process is used primarily to understand your enquiry, evaluate fit and determine an appropriate next step.

03 INFORMATION USE

WHY INFORMATION IS USED

Information may be used to respond to enquiries, review project requirements, prepare proposals, communicate about services, provide agreed work and maintain business records.

Information may also be used to improve website operation, understand service demand, prevent misuse and support internal administrative processes.

04 FORMS & COMMUNICATIONS

ENQUIRIES AND PROJECT COMMUNICATION

When you submit a website form, the information you provide may be transmitted through a form processing service and delivered to the ARVERSE business inbox.

Communications may then continue through email, calls, meetings or other agreed channels.

Please avoid sending sensitive personal information that is not relevant to the business enquiry or requested service.

05 SERVICE PROVIDERS

THIRD-PARTY SYSTEMS

ARVERSE may use external service providers for functions such as website hosting, form processing, email communication, analytics, project management and business operations.

Those providers may process information as necessary to perform the services for which they are used.

EXTERNAL PLATFORMS

Third-party platforms operate under their own privacy policies and terms. Their handling of information is governed by their respective policies.

06 DATA SECURITY

REASONABLE PROTECTION

ARVERSE takes reasonable steps to protect business and enquiry information from unauthorized access, misuse or unnecessary exposure.

However, no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

07 DATA RETENTION

HOW LONG INFORMATION MAY BE KEPT

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to enquiries, maintain project records, manage business relationships and meet legitimate operational or record-keeping requirements.

Information that is no longer reasonably required may be deleted, anonymized or archived according to operational needs.

08 YOUR CHOICES

QUESTIONS AND REQUESTS

You may contact ARVERSE regarding personal information you have directly provided through a website enquiry or business communication.

Depending on the circumstances and applicable requirements, requests may include questions about information handling, correction of inaccurate details or deletion requests.

09 POLICY UPDATES

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in website functionality, business processes, service providers or information-handling practices.

The current version published on this website will apply from the date it is made available.

10 CONTACT

PRIVACY QUESTIONS

Questions about this Privacy Policy or information submitted directly to ARVERSE can be sent through the official contact channel.
